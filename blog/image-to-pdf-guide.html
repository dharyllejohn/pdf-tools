How to Convert Images to PDF

Published 2026 • PDF Tools Pro

Introduction

Converting images into PDF is one of the easiest ways to organize photos, scanned documents, certificates and receipts into a single file. PDF documents are widely accepted for school, business and government transactions.

Why Convert Images to PDF?

  • Share multiple images in one file.
  • Print documents easily.
  • Protect formatting.
  • Store receipts and certificates.
  • Create digital portfolios.

Steps

  1. Open the Image to PDF tool.
  2. Select your JPG or PNG images.
  3. Arrange them in the correct order.
  4. Click Convert.
  5. Download the finished PDF.

Best Practices

  • Use high-quality images.
  • Rotate images before converting.
  • Arrange pages correctly.
  • Preview the PDF before downloading.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I convert PNG to PDF?

Yes. PNG, JPG and many image formats can be converted.

Is it free?

Yes. Basic image-to-PDF conversion is free.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The converter works on Android, iPhone, tablets and desktop computers.
